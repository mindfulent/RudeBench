Email Validation

Important Note on Email Validation

While regular expressions can provide a good approximation of email address validity, they cannot fully validate emails according to the RFC specifications (RFC 5322). This is because the specifications allow for a wide range of valid email address formats, including some that are not easily represented by regex patterns.

In practice, the best approach for production use is to use a combination of:

  • Regex validation for client-side formatting checks (as shown below).
  • A verification email sent to the user, which they must click to confirm their email address.

This approach ensures that the email address not only conforms to a valid format but is also actually in use.
