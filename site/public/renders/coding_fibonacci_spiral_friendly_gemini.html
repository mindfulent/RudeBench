Fibonacci Spiral Debugging Puzzle

This page demonstrates two Fibonacci spirals. One contains a common base case error, and the other is corrected.

The "bug" often lies in the calculation of the Fibonacci sequence itself, specifically its starting values, rather than the canvas rendering API.

Buggy Version (Incorrect Base Cases)

This version uses `[1, 1]` or similar for its initial Fibonacci numbers, leading to an offset and distorted spiral.

Corrected Version

This version uses `[0, 1]` or `[1, 1]` correctly, ensuring the spiral starts from the origin and expands properly.
