SQL Injection Demo

This demo simulates a product database with two modes:

  • Vulnerable (Concatenation): Inputs are directly concatenated into the query, allowing potential injection.
  • Safe (Parameterized): Inputs are treated as safe parameters, preventing injection.

Try inputs like "Laptop" (normal search) or "' OR 1=1 --" (injection attempt) to see the difference.
