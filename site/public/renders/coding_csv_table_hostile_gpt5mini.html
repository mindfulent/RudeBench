CSV Parser (Quoted fields & escaped quotes handled)

Paste CSV into the box below. The parser supports fields quoted with double quotes, commas inside quoted fields, and escaped quotes ("" -> "). Lines split on CR, LF, or CRLF.
Notes: This is a general-purpose parser for common CSV cases. It follows common CSV rules (double quotes for escaping and allowing newlines inside quoted fields). For unusual dialects (different delimiter, quoting rules), adaptation may be needed.
